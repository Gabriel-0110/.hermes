## 2. Current Trading / Profit Limitations (the truth)

1. **Gateway is stopped.** processes.json shows `[]`, gateway PID 27572 stopped, `KeepAlive=false` on launchd plists. **Crons do not auto-fire.** Strategy + research cycles only run when a human launches them. This is the single biggest profitability blocker.
2. **Channel directory empty.** channel_directory.json has no Telegram/Discord/email targets. Funding/RSI/ETH-trigger alerts are generated but **never delivered**. Bot B's $2,350 trigger will fire silently.
3. **Spot wallet has $0.05 USDT.** No dry powder. Bots C, F, G, H cannot deploy capital even when signals fire.
4. **All capital concentrated on one venue (BitMart).** Single-venue counterparty + unable to arbitrage funding spreads, basis, or cross-exchange liquidity.
5. **No SL/TP enforcement.** models.py accepts `stop_loss_price` / `take_profit_price` fields but the BitMart wrapper does not place bracket orders. Risk relies on monitor + manual exits.
6. **No position-limit enforcement.** Risk-manager has tools but the policy engine in policy_engine.py has scaffolded caps; not wired to live limits table.
7. **Strategy universe is 6 assets, 4h cadence.** Misses high-momentum alts and sub-hour reversals. No 1-minute / 15-minute scanners.
8. **Funding-rate edge is half-utilized.** funding_rate_monitor.py detects opportunities but does not generate trade proposals — it only alerts.
9. **No backtest harness used.** `replay_cases/replay_runs` tables exist but no CLI / pipeline calls them. Strategy parameters are intuitions.
10. **Copy-trader Bot E has 0 trades.** No filtering of master quality. No dynamic allocation between D and E based on rolling Sharpe.
11. **Chronos-2 forecasts are visual only.** tradingview_pine_inject.js overlays them in TradingView; `get_forecast_projection` exists as a tool but is not consumed by strategy scorers.
12. **Nansen / LunarCrush / Glassnode** all on hold — smart-money flow leg of the thesis is dark.
13. **No execution-quality feedback loop.** `execution_events` and `strategy_evaluations` are written but no nightly job updates strategy confidence priors from realized PnL.
14. **No VaR, no portfolio-level Kelly sizing.** Risk uses fixed 2 % per-trade.
15. **Orderbook data is HTTP polled.** No WebSocket for L2 — slippage detection lags.
